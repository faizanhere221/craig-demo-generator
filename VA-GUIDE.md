# 📋 Demo Site Generator - VA Guide

## Quick Start (30 Seconds)

### Step 1: Open the Generator
Go to: **[Your Demo Generator URL]**

### Step 2: Fill in the Form

| Field | What to Enter | Example |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| **Company Name** | Business name from Google Maps | Smith Roofing |
| **Phone Number** | Their phone number | 0412 345 678 |
| **Business Niche** | Select from dropdown | Roofer |
| **Location** | City/suburb, State | Sydney, NSW |

### Step 3: Click "Generate Demo Site"
Wait ~30 seconds. You'll get a live URL!

### Step 4: Copy the URL
Click "Copy" or copy the URL shown. Send it to the lead!

## 📝 Best Practices

### ✅ DO:
- Use the **exact business name** from Google Maps
- Include area code in phone numbers
- Use full location (City, State)
- Generate site BEFORE reaching out

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use abbreviations for company names
- Forget to copy the URL before closing
- Send without verifying the site loads

---

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "Generation Failed"
1. Check internet connection
2. Try shorter company name
3. Refresh page and try again

### Site Not Loading
1. Wait 30 more seconds
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser

### Wrong Information on Site
1. Double-check your inputs
2. Generate a new site with corrections
